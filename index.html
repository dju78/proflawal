üöÄ Product Development Roadmap

Phase 1: MVP (Months 1-6)

  • Instant account opening (BVN + selfie, <3 min)
  • Lightning-fast transfers (2-tap flow)
  • Leo 2.0 AI assistant (WhatsApp/Instagram integration)
  • Bill payments hub (airtime, data, electricity, cable TV)
  • Security framework (biometric, fraud detection)

Milestone: 100K active users

Phase 2: Enterprise (Months 7-12)

  • Cross-border payments (PAPSS, 20 countries)
  • Investment & savings (stocks, mutual funds, crypto on-ramp)
  • Lifestyle integration (cashback, split bills, virtual cards)
  • ESG features (carbon tracking, green savings)

Milestone: 500K active users

Phase 3: Advanced (Months 13-18)

  • Blockchain features (KYC, escrow, NFT wallet)
  • Open banking platform (API marketplace)
  • Embedded finance (e-commerce integration)

Milestone: 1M+ users, ‚Ç¶50B+ deposits
